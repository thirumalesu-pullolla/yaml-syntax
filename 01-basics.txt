Basic structure : key-value pairs

name: Alice
age: 25
is_student: yes

# Use spaces not tabs , always put a space after the colon:

# file extension: .yaml or .yml

#Lists in YAML (arrays)

fruits:
  - apple (two spaces before dash and a space after it)
  - banana
  - mango

here above fruits is a key , and the value is list.

Lists can be : 
    Strings(- apple ||  - 42 || - true || Objects)

List of Objects example:

users:
  - name: Alice
    age: 25
  - name: Bob 
    age: 30

Note:
1) we need a space after a colon
2) there are two spaces before hyphen(-)
